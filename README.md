WhatSmall

WhatSmall is an AI-powered commerce operating system designed for small businesses in Africa.

Our mission is to help merchants automate communication, sales, customer support, and daily business operations using WhatsApp and mobile-first technology.

## Vision

We believe millions of African businesses are still operating without modern digital infrastructure.

WhatSmall aims to become the intelligent business layer powering small commerce across Africa.

## Core Features

- AI WhatsApp sales assistant
- Automated customer replies
- Order management
- Customer relationship management (CRM)
- Mobile-first business tools
- AI-powered business automation

## Why Africa?

Africa has one of the fastest-growing mobile economies in the world, yet millions of businesses still rely on manual processes and informal communication tools.

WhatSmall bridges that gap with simple, accessible, and intelligent tools.

## Long-Term Goal

Build the operating system for small businesses in Africa.
